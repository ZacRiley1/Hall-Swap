What Is Hall Swap?

Each year tens of thousands of students across the UK are unhappy with the allocation of accommodation they receive from their university. As social experiences are a huge part of a student's life, feeling like you're "missing out" is worsened by today's social media hype and can even go as far as triggering depression as a consequence of a downward spiral of negative thinking.

Hall Swap creates a platform which allows students to change halls of accommodation stress and hassle free. Hall Swap uses a compatibility algorithm which matches students based on their current accommodation and preferred accommodation. The best thing is that our platform is integrated into university accommodation office systems which means that staff can manage, review and confirm swaps all in one place, making the currently cumbersome process quicker and a lot more efficient.
